Welcome.

My name is Venkata, and I am currently a first-year medical student, who is aspiring to become an interventional radiology. In addition to medicine, I enjoy bouldering, language, and photography.

About Me:

I attended high school at Loveless Academic Magnet Program in Montgomery, Alabama. Subsequently, I completed my undergraduated education at Harvard University, where I graduated magna cum laude in Computer Science and Mathematics. Currently, I am a M1 (first-year) medical student at the University of Alabama at Birmingham. My future medical interests concern Diagnostic Radiology and Interventional Radiology.
